\section*{How to Use}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Use this file as the canonical printable study document.
  \item Keep writing notes with simple ESL phrasing and case-answer structure.
  \item For exam prep, prioritize labels, reasons, risks, and recommendations.
\end{itemize}

\section*{Core Study Structure}
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Unit 1: strategy basics and decision levels.
  \item Unit 2: mission, vision, objectives, governance, agency conflict.
  \item Unit 3: PESTEL and Five Forces with causal links.
  \item Unit 4: value chain, RBV, and VRIO.
  \item Unit 5: competitive strategy, positioning, and strategy clock.
  \item Unit 6: corporate strategy, M\&A, and internationalization.
  \item Week-11: case method, argument quality, and recommendation logic.
\end{enumerate}

\section*{Exam Writing Formula}
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Label the strategy clearly.
  \item Prove label with case facts.
  \item Add external analysis point.
  \item Add internal analysis point.
  \item Explain driver and risk.
  \item End with recommendation and condition for success.
\end{enumerate}
