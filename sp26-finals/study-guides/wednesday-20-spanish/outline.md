# Spanish for Beginners - Course Outline

## Course Info
- **Date:** Wednesday 20th (10:30-13:30)
- **Duration:** 3 hours
- **Target:** Complete beginner to basic conversational

## Topics Covered

### 1. Greetings & Basic Phrases
- Essential greetings (Hola, Buenos días, etc.)
- How are you? (formal vs informal)
- Politeness (por favor, gracias, perdón)
- Goodbye variations

### 2. Personal Pronouns & Core Verbs
- Pronouns: yo, tú, usted, él, ella, nosotros, ellos
- **SER vs ESTAR:** (To be)
  - SER: identity, profession, origin, time
  - ESTAR: location, condition, feelings
- Conjugation tables for both verbs

### 3. Essential Vocabulary
- Numbers 0-20
- Days of week and months
- Common objects (casa, libro, mesa, etc.)
- Basic adjectives (grande, pequeño, rojo)
- Family members
- Food and drinks

### 4. Grammar Basics
- Gender (el/los masculine, la/las feminine)
- Articles: definite (el, la) and indefinite (un, una)
- Subject-verb agreement
- Basic word order (SVO)
- Question formation

### 5. Survival Phrases
- **Restaurant:** "Una mesa para dos", "La cuenta"
- **Hotel:** "Necesito una habitación", "¿Cuánto cuesta?"
- **Directions:** "¿Dónde está?", "Estoy perdido"
- **Shopping:** Numbers and prices

### 6. Regular Verb Conjugation
- **-AR verbs** (hablar - to speak)
- **-ER verbs** (comer - to eat)
- **-IR verbs** (vivir - to live)
- Irregular verbs preview: tener, ir, poder, querer, saber

## Quick Reference

## Focus Areas
1. Speaking (most important for beginners)
