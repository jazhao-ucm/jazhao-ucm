Week 8 — Pricing & Monetization
- **Goal:** Decide how the product makes money.
- **Pricing model:** **free / freemium / subscription / one-time / usage-based**
- **Tiers (2–3):** Name — Core benefit — Price
- **Quick experiment:** If we change price to ____ then conversion/ARPU will change by ____%.
- **Key metrics (bold):** **ARPU**, **CAC**, **LTV**, **Churn**

Week 9 — Metrics & Analytics
- **Goal:** Measure product health and growth.
- **Core funnel:** **Visitors → Signups → Activated → Paying → Retained**
- **Must-track:** **Conversion rate**, **Activation**, **Retention (7/30d)**, **Churn**
- **Quick checks to handwrite:** Conversion = ____%  •  7‑day retention = ____%

Week 10 — Launch, Feedback & Iteration
- **Goal:** Launch, collect feedback, iterate quickly.
- **Launch plan (one line per phase):** Prelaunch: ____ | Launch day: ____ | Post-launch: ____
- **Feedback sources:** **in-app**, **support**, **interviews**, **analytics**
- **Prioritize fixes:** top 3 (use Impact / Effort)
- **Primary post-launch metric:** **__________**

Notes (bottom of page):
- Weekly status: top metric • 3 wins • 3 blockers
- Quick formulas: **LTV = (ARPU × Gross Margin) / Churn Rate**
