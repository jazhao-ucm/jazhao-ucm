Product Management — Weeks 1–10 (Slides-ready Notes)

Intro: One-line purpose per week + 4–6 slide bullets. Bold items to highlight on slides.

Week 1 — Introduction & Role of PM
- **Goal:** understand product manager responsibilities.
- **Key points:** Market vs. Product vs. Engineering; **stakeholders**; roadmap basics.
- **Deliverable:** 1-sentence product mission.

Week 2 — Customer Discovery
- **Goal:** learn to find and validate customer problems.
- **Key points:** interviews, problem interviews, **JTBD**, pain vs. solution.
- **Deliverable:** 3 validated problem hypotheses.

Week 3 — Personas & Segmentation
- **Goal:** define target users and segments.
- **Key points:** primary vs secondary personas, value props per segment, **jobs-to-be-done**.
- **Deliverable:** 1‑page persona template.

Week 4 — Value Proposition & Positioning
- **Goal:** craft a clear value proposition.
- **Key points:** positioning statement: **For [who], [product] helps [benefit] because [reason]**; competitor map.
- **Deliverable:** 1‑line positioning + 3 differentiators.

Week 5 — Product Strategy & Roadmapping
- **Goal:** prioritize what to build and why.
- **Key points:** vision → goals → initiatives; prioritization methods (RICE, MoSCoW); **roadmap** types (now/next/later).
- **Deliverable:** 3-quarter roadmap sketch.

Week 6 — Design & UX Principles
- **Goal:** apply basic UX principles to features.
- **Key points:** user flows, wireframes, usability testing, **activation** heuristics.
- **Deliverable:** 1 user flow + key UX metric.

Week 7 — Go‑To‑Market (GTM)
- **Goal:** prepare Launch Plan and success criteria.
- **Key points:** target segment, **positioning**, channels, messaging, onboarding flow.
- **Deliverable:** one-page Launch Plan + primary success metric.

Week 8 — Pricing & Monetization
- **Goal:** decide how product makes money.
- **Key points:** pricing models (free, freemium, subscription, one-time, usage), tiers, anchoring, **WTP** tests.
- **Deliverable:** tier names + prices + pricing experiment hypothesis.

Week 9 — Metrics & Analytics
- **Goal:** measure product health and growth.
- **Key points:** funnel (Visitors → Signups → Activated → Paying → Retained), core metrics: **Conversion**, **Retention**, **Churn**, **LTV**, **CAC**.
- **Deliverable:** one-page dashboard sketch + cohort check.

Week 10 — Launch, Feedback & Iteration
- **Goal:** launch cleanly and iterate from feedback.
- **Key points:** launch checklist (pre/during/post), feedback channels (in-app, interviews, support), prioritize fixes (RICE), quick experiments.
- **Deliverable:** post-launch plan + 3 prioritized experiments.

Slide tips (final):
- Use 1 idea per slide; bold the single takeaway.
- Visuals: simple funnel, persona card, roadmap timeline.
- End with 3 action items and the primary metric to watch.
