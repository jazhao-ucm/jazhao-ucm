# Strategic Management - Course Outline

## Course Info
- **Date:** Thursday 21st (11:00-14:00)
- **Duration:** 3 hours

## Topics Covered

### 1. Strategy Fundamentals
- Definition: Integrated actions to exploit competencies for advantage
- Three levels: Corporate, Business, Functional
- Strategic vs Tactical vs Operational decisions

### 2. Strategic Analysis
- **SWOT Analysis:** Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats
- **Porter's Five Forces:**
  - Threat of new entrants
  - Bargaining power of suppliers
  - Bargaining power of buyers
  - Threat of substitutes
  - Rivalry among competitors

### 3. Competitive Advantage
- Cost Leadership (lowest cost)
- Differentiation (unique features/quality)
- Focus/Niche (dominate segment)
- **VRIO Framework:** Valuable, Rare, Inimitable, Organized
- Sustainable vs temporary advantage

### 4. Business Models
- Business Model Canvas components
- Common models: Freemium, Subscription, Marketplace, Platform
- Creating and capturing value

### 5. Strategy Formulation
- Environmental analysis
- Strategic objectives setting
- **Ansoff Matrix:**
  - Market penetration
  - Product development
  - Market development
  - Diversification
- **Porter's Generic Strategies:** Cost leadership, differentiation, focus

### 6. Implementation
- Organizational structure alignment
- Resource allocation
- **Balanced Scorecard:** Financial, Customer, Internal Process, Learning
- Change management and execution
- Performance measurement

### 7. Corporate & Portfolio Strategy
- Vertical integration (forward/backward)
- Horizontal integration (M&A)
- Related vs unrelated diversification
- **BCG Matrix:** Stars, Cash Cows, Question Marks, Dogs

### 8. Case Study: TechCore Strategic Pivot
- Legacy software company facing disruption
- Strategic options analysis
- Pivot vs acquisition vs evolution

## Key Frameworks Summary
- SWOT, Porter's 5 Forces, VRIO, Ansoff, BCG, Balanced Scorecard

## Strategic Planning Pyramid
1. Mission & Vision (Why & Where)
2. Strategic Objectives (What)
3. Strategies (How)
4. Tactics (Specific actions)
5. Operations (Daily execution)
