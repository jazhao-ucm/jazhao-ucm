## ğŸ¯ Course Objectives

This course provides students with:
- Understanding of different national management styles for competitive advantage in global markets
- Knowledge of how organizations add value in multicultural environments
- Skills to address cultural differences in international business situations
- Insights into organizational behavior, business strategy, HR management, ethics, and communication in international contexts
- Cultural competence and cross-cultural communication skills

**Key Focus:** How corporate managers can align company mission with consumer expectations while ensuring organizational development and success across different cultural contexts.
