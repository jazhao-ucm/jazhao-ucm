**Level:** B1 (Intermediate)

## üéØ Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, students will be able to:
- Understand the main points of clear standard input on familiar matters
- Deal with most situations likely to arise while traveling
- Produce simple connected text on familiar topics
- Describe experiences, events, dreams, hopes, and ambitions
- Give reasons and explanations for opinions and plans

## üí° Teaching Tips

1. **Always in Spanish**: Use Spanish as much as possible in class
2. **Error Correction**: Focus on communication first, accuracy second
3. **Pair Work**: Maximize speaking time through pair activities
4. **Real Materials**: Use authentic materials (news, videos, etc.)
5. **Cultural Context**: Include cultural information in lessons
6. **Student-Centered**: Let students lead discussions
7. **Variety**: Mix activities to keep engagement high
8. **Encouragement**: Create a positive, supportive environment
