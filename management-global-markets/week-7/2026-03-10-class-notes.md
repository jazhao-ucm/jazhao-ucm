| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **4.1** | Introduction. Scope, objective, idea/improvement. Competitive advantages. |
| **4.2** | Company description. Name, type, location, products/services, mission, values, value proposition. **Business Model Canvas.** |

| **4.4** | Competitive analysis. |
| **4.5** | Strategic plan. |
| **4.6** | Contingency plan. |
| **4.7** | Marketing plan. Market research. 4P's. **Calendar.** **Budget Year 1.** |

**Preliminaries:** Front page (faculty, title, students, tutor, date), copyright, table of contents, **executive summary** (~1 page, like a thesis abstract).

## III. Key Frameworks (Quick Reference)

- **PESTLE:** Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, Environmental (macro).
- **SWOT:** Strengths, Weaknesses (internal), Opportunities, Threats (external).
- **Strategic plan:** Current status → future focus. Short-term (0–12 months), long-term (1–3 years).
- **Contingency plan:** “What if?” scenarios and responses.
- **Marketing plan:** 4P's (Product, Price, Place, Promotion); month-by-month calendar; Year 1 budget.
