### 3. Employee Selection: Headquarters vs. Local

**The Key Question:** "When I say define, take with us the four employees from headquarters, or we hire people from local market?"

**Option 1: Bring People from Headquarters**
- **Pros:**
  - Know the company culture
  - Understand the business
  - Trusted by headquarters
  - Protect company interests
  - Already trained
- **Cons:**
  - More expensive to move
  - May not understand local market
  - Cultural barriers

**Option 2: Hire People from Local Market**
- **Pros:**
  - Understand local culture
  - Speak local language
  - Know local market
  - Cheaper (no relocation costs)
  - Better for local relationships
- **Cons:**
  - May not understand company culture
  - Need training
  - May not protect company interests
  - Takes time to train

**Key Decision Factors:**
- **Technology level:** If company has high technology, may need to bring experts
- **Education level:** If local market lacks education, may need to bring people
- **Competition:** If moving to compete, may need experienced people
- **Cost:** Bringing people is expensive

**Key Insight:** "It is better to hire people from the local market. Yes or not? It makes sense. It doesn't make sense for all of you, yes or not? Can make you. Make sense."

**Example:**
- France to Chile: Moved people, moved parents, moved secure employees
- More expensive to move
- Better to hire locally for better feasibility
