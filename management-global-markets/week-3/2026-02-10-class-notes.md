III. Employee Selection: Headquarters vs. Local
   A. The Key Question
      1. "Bring employees from headquarters or hire locally?"
   B. Option 1: Bring People from Headquarters
      1. Pros:
         a. Know company culture
         b. Understand the business
         c. Trusted by headquarters
         d. Protect company interests
         e. Already trained
      2. Cons:
         a. More expensive to move
         b. May not understand local market
         c. Cultural barriers
   C. Option 2: Hire People from Local Market
      1. Pros:
         a. Understand local culture
         b. Speak local language
         c. Know local market
         d. Cheaper (no relocation costs)
         e. Better for local relationships
      2. Cons:
         a. May not understand company culture
         b. Need training
         c. May not protect company interests
         d. Takes time to train
   D. Key Decision Factors
      1. Technology level: high tech may need experts
      2. Education level: low education may need HQ people
      3. Competition: may need experienced people
      4. Cost: bringing people is expensive
   E. Example
      1. France to Chile: moved people, moved parents, moved secure employees
      2. More expensive to move
      3. Better to hire locally for feasibility

IV. Training vs. Bringing People
   A. Training Option
      1. Train local people
      2. Takes time
      3. "Too long to train engineers"
   B. Bringing People Option
      1. Bring experienced from headquarters
      2. Faster
      3. Already know the business
      4. Protect company interests
   C. Key Point
      1. "Those people from company protect company interests"
   D. Reality
      1. Depends on situation
      2. Sometimes need both

VI. The Two Jobs of a Manager
   A. Job 1: Keep an Eye on the Capital
      1. Protect the money
      2. Monitor spending
      3. Control expenses
   B. Job 2: Put Money on the Table
      1. Find money
      2. Create growth
      3. Generate revenue
   C. Key Principle
      1. "If you lose capital, you lose your job"
   D. Reality
      1. Must balance both
      2. Protect capital AND create growth

      3. More important than age
