# Introduction to the EU - Week 10 Vocabulary A-Z

- **Acquis Communautaire** - Body of EU law and obligations member states adopt.
- **Agency (EU)** - Specialized EU body supporting implementation and coordination.
- **Citizenship (EU)** - Rights attached to being a national of an EU member state.
- **Cohesion Policy** - EU policy reducing regional economic disparities.
- **Commission** - EU institution that proposes legislation and oversees implementation.
- **Court of Justice (CJEU)** - Judicial authority interpreting EU law.
- **Decision-Making (EU)** - Institutional process for adopting EU legal acts.
- **Economic and Monetary Union (EMU)** - Integration framework for economic policy and common currency.
- **Enlargement** - Process by which new states join the EU.
- **European Central Bank (ECB)** - Institution responsible for euro area monetary policy.
- **European Council** - Body of heads of state/government setting broad EU direction.
- **Free Movement** - Right to move, live, work, and study across member states.
- **Green Deal** - EU strategy for climate-neutral and sustainable transformation.
- **Identity (European)** - Political/cultural dimension of belonging to the EU project.
- **Integration Through Law** - Expansion/deepening of integration via legal rules and courts.
- **Migration Policy** - EU and national rules managing mobility and migration.
- **Monetary Policy** - Interest-rate and liquidity decisions influencing inflation/growth.
- **Primacy of EU Law** - Principle that EU law takes precedence over conflicting national law.
- **Rule of Law** - Requirement that public power is exercised under legal constraints.
- **Single Market** - Space of free movement for goods, services, capital, and people.
