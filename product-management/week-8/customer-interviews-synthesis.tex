\section*{Person One: Karol}
\textbf{Q: How do you usually discover or meet new people?}\\
\textbf{A:} Mostly from friends, like in pregame or when somebody brings new people. I do not go alone to talk with strangers. For me it is better when it happens in a natural way.

\textbf{Q: How did you figure out whether you wanted to stay in touch with people you met for the first time?}\\
\textbf{A:} For me it is about feeling. If I feel calm and comfortable, I stay in contact. If I finish the talk and I am overthinking too much, normally I do not continue.

\textbf{Q: What makes meeting new people meaningful for you?}\\
\textbf{A:} When it feels real. I do not need to speak about deep things in first minute, but I want to feel the person is authentic, not only doing fake small talk.

\textbf{Q: When was the last time you met someone in a structured way? How did you feel about it?}\\
\textbf{A:} In class, with group work. In the beginning I am shy, a bit closed, but after some minutes I feel more relaxed.

\textbf{Q: What traits do you look for in new people?}\\
\textbf{A:} Kind people, calm vibe, and people that do not put pressure.

\textbf{Q: How do you usually find out about events, plans, or things happening around you? Tell me about a recent example.}\\
\textbf{A:} Almost always by friends and WhatsApp groups. One friend told me ``pregame tonight'' and I went.

\section*{Person Three: Laura}
\textbf{Q: How do you usually discover or meet new people?}\\

\textbf{Q: How did you figure out whether you wanted to stay in touch with people you met for the first time?}\\

\textbf{Q: When was the last time you met someone in a structured way? How did you feel about it?}\\

\textbf{Q: What traits do you look for in new people?}\\

\textbf{Q: How do you usually find out about events, plans, or things happening around you? Tell me about a recent example.}\\

\section*{Person Four: Mimi}
\textbf{Q: How do you usually discover or meet new people?}\\

\textbf{Q: How did you figure out whether you wanted to stay in touch with people you met for the first time?}\\

\textbf{Q: What traits do you look for in new people?}\\

\textbf{Q: How do you usually find out about events, plans, or things happening around you? Tell me about a recent example.}\\

\section*{Cross-Interview Pattern Snapshot}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \textbf{Discovery:} New people are usually met through trusted social bridges, not cold outreach.
  \item \textbf{Stay-in-touch filter:} Comfort and conversational ease are stronger filters than status or image.
  \item \textbf{Meaning:} Authenticity matters more than intensity.
  \item \textbf{Structured settings:} Useful for some, draining for others; context and personality shape the outcome.
  \item \textbf{Event channels:} Group chats and friend messages remain the dominant discovery path.
\end{itemize}
