# Spanish for Beginners - Week 14 Vocabulary A-Z

- **Adjetivo** - Word that describes or qualifies a noun.
- **Articulo** - Determiner before nouns indicating gender and number (el, la, los, las).
- **Concordancia** - Agreement between noun-adjective/article in gender and number.
- **Conjugacion** - Changing a verb form by person, tense, and mood.
- **Demostrativo** - Pointer words like este, ese, aquel.
- **Determinante** - Word introducing a noun (article, demonstrative, possessive, etc.).
- **Entonacion** - Intonation pattern shaping meaning in spoken sentences.
- **Futuro Proximo** - Near-future form built with ir + a + infinitive.
- **Genero** - Grammatical classification as masculine or feminine.
- **Gerundio** - Verb form ending in -ando/-iendo used for ongoing actions.
- **Haber** - Auxiliary verb used to build compound tenses (he comido, hemos visto).
- **Imperativo** - Command form for instructions, requests, or advice.
- **Imperfecto** - Past tense for habitual, ongoing, or descriptive actions.
- **Indicativo** - Mood used for factual statements and real situations.
- **Infinitivo** - Base verb form ending in -ar, -er, or -ir.
- **Interrogativo** - Question words such as que, donde, cuando, como, por que.
- **Irregular** - Verb pattern that does not follow regular endings.
- **Negacion** - Structure expressing no/not/never.
- **Objeto Directo** - Noun/pronoun directly receiving the action.
- **Objeto Indirecto** - Noun/pronoun receiving benefit or recipient role.
- **Participio** - Past participle form used with haber and as adjective in some contexts.
- **Preposicion** - Linking word such as de, a, con, para, por.
- **Presente** - Tense used for current actions, routines, and general truths.
- **Pronombre** - Word replacing a noun (yo, tu, lo, me, etc.).
- **Reflexivo** - Verb/pronoun structure where subject acts on itself.
- **Ser vs Estar** - Contrast between permanent identity/characteristics and temporary states/locations.
- **Sujeto** - Person or thing performing the action.
- **Sustantivo** - Noun naming people, places, objects, or ideas.
- **Tiempo Verbal** - Verb tense indicating when action occurs.
- **Verbo** - Word expressing action, process, or state.
- **Vocabulario** - Word set used for comprehension and communication.

## A1 Survival Vocabulary (Core Set)

### 1) Greetings and Courtesies
- **Hola** - Hello
- **Buenos días** - Good morning
- **Buenas tardes** - Good afternoon
- **Buenas noches** - Good evening / good night
- **¿Cómo estás?** - How are you? (informal)
- **Encantado/a** - Nice to meet you
- **Por favor** - Please
- **Gracias** - Thank you
- **De nada** - You’re welcome
- **Lo siento** - I’m sorry
- **Perdón** - Excuse me / sorry

### 2) Personal Information
- **Nombre** - First name
- **Apellido** - Last name
- **Edad** - Age
- **Nacionalidad** - Nationality
- **Profesión** - Profession
- **Dirección** - Address
- **Teléfono** - Phone number
- **Estado civil** - Marital status
- **Soltero/a** - Single
- **Casado/a** - Married

### 3) Numbers (0–100)
- **0–10:** cero, uno, dos, tres, cuatro, cinco, seis, siete, ocho, nueve, diez
- **11–20:** once, doce, trece, catorce, quince, dieciséis, diecisiete, dieciocho, diecinueve, veinte
- **Tens / multiples of 10:** treinta (30), cuarenta (40), cincuenta (50), sesenta (60), setenta (70), ochenta (80), noventa (90), cien (100)

### 4) Time, Days, and Weather
- **Lunes, Martes, Miércoles, Jueves, Viernes, Sábado, Domingo** - Days of the week
- **Enero, Febrero, Marzo, Abril, Mayo, Junio, Julio, Agosto, Septiembre, Octubre, Noviembre, Diciembre** - Months
- **Hoy / Mañana / Ayer** - Today / Tomorrow / Yesterday
- **Hace sol** - It’s sunny
- **Hace frío** - It’s cold
- **Llueve** - It’s raining
- **Hace calor** - It’s hot

### 5) The Family (La Familia)
- **Padre / Madre** - Father / Mother
- **Hermano / Hermana** - Brother / Sister
- **Hijo / Hija** - Son / Daughter
- **Abuelo / Abuela** - Grandfather / Grandmother

### 6) Food and Places in the City
- **Comida (core items):** pan (bread), agua (water), café (coffee), leche (milk), fruta (fruit), carne (meat)
- **Lugares (core places):** el supermercado, el banco, la farmacia, la estación de metro, el restaurante, la calle

### 7) Question Words (Las Interrogativas)
- **¿Qué?** - What?
- **¿Quién?** - Who?
- **¿Dónde?** - Where?
- **¿Cuándo?** - When?
- **¿Cuánto?** - How much?
- **¿Cómo?** - How?

### 8) Essential Verbs (Present Tense Core)
- **ser / estar** - to be
- **tener** - to have
- **hacer** - to do / make
- **ir** - to go
- **comer / beber** - to eat / to drink
- **hablar** - to speak
- **vivir** - to live
