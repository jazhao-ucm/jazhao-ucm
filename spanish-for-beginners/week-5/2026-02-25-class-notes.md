## IV. Exam tips

A. Agreement: Nacionalidad & profesión match person (m/f)
B. 6 = seis. quién = qu (not q)
C. Listening: Focus on name, origin, nationality, profession, age
D. Gapfill: Read full sentence for context. Check gender (mi amiga → -a)
E. Por/para: Infinitive after? → para. Noun after? → por. Clause with verb? → porque
