IV. Exam Tips
   A. Agreement
      1. Nacionalidad & profesión match person (m/f)
   B. Spelling
      1. 6 = seis
      2. Quién = qu (not just q)
   C. Listening Strategy
      1. Focus on name
      2. Focus on origin
      3. Focus on nationality
      4. Focus on profession
      5. Focus on age
   D. Gapfill Strategy
      1. Read full sentence for context
      2. Check gender (mi amiga → -a)
   E. Por/Para Rule
      1. Infinitive after? → para
      2. Noun after? → por
      3. Clause with verb? → porque
