## Key Takeaways

1. Assess community needs first - Don't build before understanding what people want
2. Testing as investing - Testing is part of development, not separate
3. Build community before product - Community comes first
4. Don't assume people will pay - Test willingness to pay early
5. Iterate based on feedback - Each cycle develops something new
6. Focus investment - Invest in what matters most, not everything
7. Understand the problem - Don't build solutions to problems that don't exist
8. Pivot when needed - If no one wants to pay, change the model
9. Partnerships can help - Work with universities, corporations
10. Free can work - Google model: free service, paid through advertising
11. Branding matters - Strong brands need less information
12. Success metrics matter - Define what success looks like
13. Service quality varies - Manage expectations
14. Real world > theory - Application matters more than perfect theory
