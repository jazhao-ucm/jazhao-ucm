## Key Takeaways

1. Assess community needs first - Don't build before understanding what people want
2. Personal problems â‰  market problems - Just because you have a problem doesn't mean others do
3. Test willingness to pay early - Before building, test if people will pay
4. Iterate based on feedback - Each cycle develops something new
5. Focus investment - Invest in what matters most, not everything
6. Platform choice matters - iOS vs. Android affects design and user base
7. Pivot when needed - If no one wants to pay, change the model
8. Partnerships can help - Work with universities, corporations
9. Free can work - Google model: free service, paid through advertising
10. Success metrics matter - Define what success looks like
11. Service quality varies - Manage expectations
12. Real world > theory - Application matters more than perfect theory
13. Brand investment - Can be expensive but effective (Super Bowl example)
14. Community before product - Build community first, then product
