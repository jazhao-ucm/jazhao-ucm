### 6. Responsive Production Systems

**Key Concept:** Production systems that respond to actual demand rather than forecasts

**Characteristics:**
- **Small initial batches:** Test the market
- **Monitor sales:** Track what's selling
- **Adjust production:** Increase if selling, stop if not
- **Quick turnaround:** Fast from design to store

**Benefits:**
- Less waste
- Better alignment with customer demand
- Can catch trends quickly

**Challenges:**
- More complex than forecast-based systems
- Requires good communication systems
- Need responsive supply chains

**Key Insight:** "So basically you do, they. Now I. So I mean, are they class? Are they weak?"

### 7. Business Model Trade-offs

**Standardization vs. Customization:**
- Standardization = Efficiency, speed, lower costs
- Customization = Customer satisfaction, but slower and more expensive

**Forecast-based vs. Responsive:**
- Forecast-based = Predictable, efficient, but can create waste
- Responsive = Less waste, better alignment, but more complex

**Speed vs. Quality:**
- Fast food prioritizes speed
- Fine dining prioritizes quality and customization

**Key Insight:** Different business models require different production strategies.
